Alumni

Alumni Mailing List

As a chapter, we host a few events a year that we like to invite our alumni to such as Alumni Tailgate, and we also have some big events like our centennial. If you would like to receive information about our chapter's alumni events, please update your information.

Endowment

We also welcome any donations to our endowment fund which we use primarily for scholarships for our members. To donate, please click the icon.

Tau Beta Pi Nationals

Nationals would also like to have up to date information about all of its members, so they would appreciate if you tell them if you move or your information changes. You can do that by sending an email with your information to: member.update@tbp.org.
