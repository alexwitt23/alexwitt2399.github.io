Actives

Requirements

6 points, at least 3 of which are from meetings, 1 of which is an active meeting.

Actives' Points

Family Actives

We send a form out at the beginning of the semester to sign up to be a family active. Any active can sign up to be a family active.

As a family active, you will be put on a team with candidates, officers, and other actives. You will get the opportunity to meet and bond with other members while also competing to be the best family.

Stoles and Cords

TBP has cords and stoles available for actives to wear at their graduation if they participate in at least 6 hours of TBP events the semester they are graduating.

Cords and stoles cost $40 which you can pay to any officer in the TBP Office, but if you return the cords and stoles after graduation, you will receive $30 back.
